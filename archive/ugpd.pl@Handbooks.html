Handbooks

  • Hypertext UC Berkeley PDP TA Handbook, by Eric Hsu. This is one of the earliest (but still useful) online TA handbooks for the original Treisman workshop at UC Berkeley.
  • Chalking It Up: Advice to a New TA, Third Edition, by Bruce Reznick, University of Illinois. This is the revised edition of the teaching guide written by Bruce Reznick for the 1984 grad student orientation at the University of Illinois. Sections include Preparing, Lecturing, Grading, and Coping.
