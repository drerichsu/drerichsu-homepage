Mailing Lists

  • The Preparing and Supporting Teachers of Undergraduate Mathematics (PSTUM) mailing list is designed to facilitate communication and collaboration among higher education faculty members, administrators, and students involved with the preparation, training, support, mentoring, supervision, and development of teachers of undergraduate mathematics, particularly those new to teaching such as graduate students and junior faculty. Some possible uses of the list include sharing ideas, techniques, reflections, successes, cautions, or news and seeking references or information.
